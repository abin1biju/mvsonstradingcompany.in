Types

There are mainly three types of cement BOPP,HDPE,PPC and we are providing a special cement called DSP

BOPP

Biaxially oriented polypropylene (BOPP) is a durable material cover that is commonly used for packaging bird seed, pet food, deer corn, and animal nutrition products. It is also the packaging of choice for rice and minerals due to its strength and ability to hold weight without breaking easily.
